Comprehensive Year-End Sales and Performance Analysis for Universal Export

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of Universal Export, a leading UK-based garment manufacturer, focusing on its operational performance in 2023. The goal was to derive data-driven insights to inform strategic decisions and enhance stakeholder communication.

The analysis utilized a complex dataset from 'GarmentFactoryDataset.zip', containing five files in various formats (xlsx, csv, json, txt). These files encompassed crucial information on transactions, customers, products, logistics, and sales personnel.

The project methodology involved:

  • Data Preparation:
    Meticulous extraction, cleaning, and transformation of data, including standardization of column names and handling of missing or erroneous data.
  • Data Modeling:
    Establishing relationships between the five main tables to create a robust star schema model, enabling comprehensive cross-table analysis.
  • Visualization and Analysis:
    Developing interactive dashboards and reports using Power BI, with an accessible color-safe theme for enhanced readability.
  • Dual Reporting Approach:
    Creation of two distinct reports:
    • An External Business Operations Report for shareholders, covering overall performance, product portfolio, geographical market analysis, and sustainability initiatives.
    • An Internal Salespeople Report, providing insights into sales performance, customer acquisition trends, and product profitability margins.

The project demonstrated proficiency in data handling, advanced analytics, and business intelligence tools. It showcased the ability to translate complex data into clear, actionable insights for different stakeholders.

Key areas of analysis included sales and profit trends, product portfolio strengths and weaknesses, geographical performance, sustainability efforts, salesperson performance, customer acquisition, and margin analysis.

This comprehensive approach provided Universal Export with valuable insights to guide future strategies, optimize operations, and communicate effectively with both internal teams and external stakeholders.
